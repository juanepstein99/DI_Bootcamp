# US Superstore Data Visualization

# Key Findings
- Sales increased over the years, showing overall business growth.
- A small number of products generate most of the total sales.
- Higher discounts are usually associated with lower profit, and sometimes negative profit.
- Sales are concentrated in specific regions, meaning the business depends more on certain markets.

# Visualizations
- Line chart showing sales trends over time
- Bar chart showing top products by sales
- Scatter plot showing the relationship between profit and discount

# Conclusion
The analysis shows that sales are increasing, but high discounts reduce profitability. Data visualization helps understand trends and relationships in the data.
